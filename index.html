Signature Dishes

Our most loved creations — made fresh daily with love and the finest spices

The Famous Pilau Dish

A fragrant blend of long-grain rice slow-cooked with cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and tender marinated beef. Inspired by the rich Swahili coast tradition, our pilau is served with fresh kachumbari and creamy coconut sauce for a perfectly balanced finish.

Chef’s Special Tamarind Fish Curry

Fresh catch simmered gently in a velvety coconut curry infused with turmeric, coriander, garlic, and a hint of tamarind. Slow-cooked to perfection and served with fragrant rice or warm chapati for a deeply comforting coastal experience.

Velvety Spiced Pumpkin Cream Soup

A silky blend of slow-roasted pumpkin, caramelized onions, and warm African spices, finished with a swirl of fresh cream. Smooth, comforting, and delicately balanced — the perfect start to your dining experience.

About Us|Our story

Sereya Spice Kitchen was born from a passion for preserving authentic regional African recipes while embracing the warmth of family dining. We source the finest whole spices, grind them fresh daily, and prepare every dish with care — just like home cooking in Kenya. Whether you're craving comfort food or celebrating a special occasion, we invite you to experience the true taste of Africa.
